Irbid is a Jordanian city , the center of the Irbid Governorate in northern Jordan and its largest city. It is located 71 km north of the capital , Amman , and about 20 km south of the Syrian-Jordanian border . It is the third largest city in the Kingdom after the Jordanian capital and Zarqa in terms of population. The population of the city and its suburbs is more than 555,000, according to the estimated population census for the year 2019, and the area of ​​the city and its suburbs is estimated at about 30 square kilometers. The location of the city of Irbid is considered average compared to the rest of the governorate areas . It is located at latitude 32 North and longitude 35 East. The geographical nature of the city is characterized by the plain nature , although the western regions of it are of a closer natureof mountainous nature .
